Summary

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a Ph.D candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of McGill University, Canada. I am a member of ISIP Lab, under the supervision of Professor Warren J. Gross.

Prior to joining McGill I obtained my M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees at Seoul National University, South Korea and Posts & Telecommunications Institute of Technology, Vietnam in 2017 and 2014, respectively. All of my degrees are in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My primary research interests include customized deep-learning algorithm design, hardware-aware algorithm optimization, and VLSI implementation for channel coding and digital signal processing applications.
